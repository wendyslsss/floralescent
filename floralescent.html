About Floralescent

Official Logo

A capstone research product developed by the members of A12-01 (Group 1). Floralescent is a flower-inspired mosquito repellent where beauty meets protection, designed to combine functionality, sustainability, and innovation.

Product Overview

The product features a bamboo-wood base and an Arduino-powered smart sprayer system that releases citronella and lemon eucalyptus, both proven to effectively repel mosquitoes and flies. With its eco-friendly materials and modern design, Floralescent offers a sustainable solution for insect control while maintaining an elegant, decorative appearance suitable for homes and shared spaces.
